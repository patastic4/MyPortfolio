LStore database

The overall goal of this project was to replicate the Lineage-based Data Store(L-Store) created by our Professor Mohammad Sadoghi. A database that aims to manage data that is constantly being updated and at the same time analyzing large sets of data, using a lineage-based storage architecture. You can read more about this Database and its design here
[ L-Store: A Real-time OLTP and OLAP System ].

The Sultans of Suggestion

The game genre is a roguelike 2D bullet hell game, where the player starts in a dungeon room and needs to beat the boss. The player will progress through rooms and gain upgrades until they reach the final boss. These upgrades will be represented in card format and there will both be generally positive options as well as other options that are more chaotic and have some extra kind of negative effect. Mobs that must be slain to progress will spawn in rooms as the player progresses, slowly ascending in difficulty as they approach the boss.

Credit Card Approval

Here my group and I proposed a machine learning model to help applicants gauge their credit worthiness before applying for a credit card, helping them avoid an unnecessary credit score drop from a hard pull on their credit report. In this project we trained four supervised learning models; Logistic Regression, XGBoost(Decision Tree), Multi-layer Perception, and a Support Vector Machine(SVM).
